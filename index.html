About Us

Our mission is to establish sustainable, eco-friendly practices across towns and cities to improve air quality, reduce pollution, and create healthier communities.

Vision: An "Oxygen Country" where clean air, green spaces, and sustainable practices are part of everyday life.

Key Initiatives

  • Green Spaces in Public Areas: Plant oxygen-rich and herbal trees in public areas like offices, bus stops, and railway stations.
  • Roadside Tree Planting: Increase tree coverage along roads to combat pollution and beautify cities.
  • Green Housing Development: Dedicate 10% of housing project land to tree planting and water conservation pits.
  • Plastic Ban: Ban single-use plastics and promote sustainable materials.
  • Promotion of Electric Vehicles: Incentivize EVs and reduce fossil fuel consumption.
  • Smoke-Free Zones: Establish no-smoking areas for a cleaner environment.
  • Effective Drainage Systems: Maintain proper drainage to prevent flooding and promote cleanliness.
  • Eco-Friendly Advertising: Replace plastic flexes with sustainable materials for advertisements.

Technical Approach

  • Government and Private Partnerships: Collaborate to fund green infrastructure and sustainable initiatives.
  • Community Engagement: Educate citizens on green living and encourage participation in urban greening projects.
  • Regular Monitoring: Use technology to monitor air quality and ensure continuous improvement.
